🌍 ABOUT

Velaris is a sci-fi strategy game about the survival of civilization on an unknown planet. A cooperative effort to give humanity a second chance after self-destruction. A continuous story driven by the success of the entire game community. Shared challenges, known as “world challenges”, bring a new type of MMO experience – shared city building, economic cycles. Technology draws from the remnants of a lost civilization and human ingenuity – you must understand both to succeed.
