Community Rules

Players are part of the Veralis community and are therefore expected to follow these behavioral guidelines:

1. Basic Behavior

  • Respect other players and developers.
  • No hate speech, bullying or harassment.
  • No spam, advertising or flooding.

2. Multiple Accounts

Having more than one account per person is allowed only if not abused (e.g., resource transfer, cooldown circumvention). Violations may lead to account bans.
